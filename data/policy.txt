Company HR Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.

Sick leave allowance is 10 days per year.

Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Employees must submit expense reports within 30 days of purchase.

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.

Training budgets are allocated at $1500 per employee annually.

Customer support operates 24/7 across global regions.

Engineering teams follow an agile development process with two-week sprints.

Security policy requires all employees to enable two-factor authentication.
